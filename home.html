WE PROVIDE PEACE!

Everyone deserves to be able to change and grow in peace. Dvyne Soulz aims to be the guiding light amongst the chaos and confusion by providing information and resources to better our community.

COME AS YOU ARE AND LEAVE WITH KNOWLEDGE OF SELF!

The greatest thing an individual can achieve in their lifetime is acquiring a thorough knowledge of self. Embarking on this journey of self-discovery leads individuals to begin exploring who they are, where they come from and where they are going.
Knowledge of self helps to establish a good, orderly direction that inspires the individual to strive towards being a better person with the will to achieve whatever their mind can conceive. Knowledge of self causes an individual to develop a heightened sense of awareness in regard to the community in which they live, initiating civic engagement for the purpose of making a positive change and becoming an asset to the community.

OUR GOAL

To provide resources that assist with developing life skills, to utilize education to empower, to help establish a more positive role
for men in order to strengthen community and to implement a curriculum that targets and begins a process of healing the behavioral issues that have been identified as a main root cause of many of the problems that our men and children face.
