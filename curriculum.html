Tier 1 - N.O.W.
No One Waits is focused on building strong relationships, finding a connection and using that connection to utilize Restorative Practice Moments, and explore identity. Growth is contingent on trust.

At this tier we measure:
  1. The number of interactions between client and staff.
  2. Requests for help by clients
  3. Number of visits to facility.
  4. Completion of intake and/or assessment forms.
Tier 2 - I.A.P
Individual Accountability Progress is focused on identifying the individual's short-term motivators, long-term motivators and providing transformative mentorship.

At this tier we measure:
  1. Progress on I.A.P.
  2. Progress on the identification of short-term and long-term motivators.
Tier 3 - K.D.B
Knock Down Barriers is focused on providing vital resources and basic needs support (food insecurities, transportation needs, etc...), education, health & wellness (nutrition, free flu shots, physicals, mobile eye exams, community service opportunities), enhancing economic empowerment and providing employment resources.

At this tier we measure:
  1. The number of requests for resources.
  2. The number of job applications completed and turned in.
  3. Access to health and wellness.
  4. Connections in community made / supports identified.
Tier 4 - T.E.S.E.
Transformation-Enrichment-Social Skills Building-Emotional Skills Building is focused on transformation which provides a knowledge of self. Critical mentoring will be provided to build on Social Skills and Emotional Skills Building. Enrichment will allow youth to explore athletic potentials, career explorations, college exploration, and to ensure youth are academically on grade level to graduate high school.

At this tier we measure:
  1. Knowledge of self.
  2. Interactions with staff to build social skills and emotional skills.
  3. Number of visits to Dvyne Soulz (to utilize explorations).
  4. Graduation numbers.
  5. Employment numbers.

Music Wellness Path:
Tier 1 - N.O.W.
No One Waits is focused on using Music Wellness to strengthen relationships, finding a connection and using that connection to utilize Restorative Practice Moments and explore identity.

At this tier we measure:
  1. The number of interactions between client and staff.
  2. Requests for help by clients
  3. Number of visits to facility.
  4. Completion of intake and/or assessment forms.
Tier 2 - I.A.P
Individual Accountability Progress is focused on identifying the individual's short-term motivators, long-term motivators and providing transformative "musicship". Music Wellness can address a variety of wellness and educational motivators. Using different kinds of musical experiences, such as singing, playing instruments, writing songs and listening to and talking about music can assist in identifying motivators.

At this tier we measure:
  1. Progress on I.A.P.
  2. Progress on the identification of short-term and long-term motivators.
Tier 3 - K.D.B
Knock Down Barriers is focused on using Music Wellness to promote wellness, manage stress, and improve communication. Music Wellness can assist with physical and psychological issues. If offers a variety of creative expressions that can be tailored to clients facing different problems.

At this tier we measure:
  1. The number of requests for resources.
  2. The number of job applications completed and turned in.
  3. Access to health and wellness.
  4. Connections in community made / supports identified.
Tier 4 - T.E.S.E.
Transformation-Enrichment-Social Skills Building-Emotional Skills Building is focused on the skill building benefits of Music Wellness which can include the following: reduces social anxiety, expression of grief and other traumatic feelings, curbs impulsivity, lowers blood pressure, relaxes muscles, improves fine motor skills, aids insomnia issues, reduces headaches, and can assist healing for patients with traumatic brain injuries. Connecting the music to yourself is important before you can connect it to others.

At this tier we measure:
  1. Knowledge of self.
  2. Interactions with staff to build social skills and emotional skills.
  3. Number of visits to Dvyne Soulz (to utilize explorations).
  4. Graduation numbers.
  5. Employment numbers.
